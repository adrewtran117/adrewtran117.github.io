About Me

i'm an honors undergraduate student at Temple University studying Computer Science. This summer, I'm a software development engineer at Amazon Web Services (AWS).

I've served as President of the Temple University Association of Computing Machinery Chapter and Director of OwlHacks, Temple University's annual hackathon.
